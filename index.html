June 10, 2021

Armed gunmen, including Palestinian Authority (PA) security officers, confront Israeli security forces searching for two men who shot at Israeli soldiers. Two PA security officers and the founder of Islamic Jihad’s Jenin branch are killed in a shootout. 

March 22, 2022

Muhammad Galeb Ahmad Abu Alqian stabs and kills three Israelis, then rams and kills a fourth with his car in the southern Israeli city of Beersheba.

March 27, 2022

Two members of the Islamic State, Ayman and Khaled Ighbariya, shoot two Israeli police officers.

March 29, 2022

Dia Hamarsheh, from the West Bank village of Ya’bad, enters Israel illegally and kills four civilians and a police officer in Bnei Brak.

March 31, 2022

 The Israel Defense Forces (IDF) launches operation “Wave Breaker” in response to the increase in terrorist attacks.

April 8, 2022

Raad Khazem, from Jenin, murders three people in Tel Aviv before being killed by Israeli security forces

April 29, 2022

Yousef Sameeh Assi and Yahya Marei murder a security guard in the West Bank community of Ariel.

May 5, 2022

As’ad Rifa’i and Subhi Sbeihat, both from the West Bank village of Rummanah, stab to death three people in Elad.
